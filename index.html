World Heritage in your pocket

Discover the cultural, historical and natural heritage right around you. Wherever you are, find nearby churches, palaces, gardens, museums, monuments and viewpoints — with photos, information and an automatic audio guide.

Search for Patrimonia 360 on the Google Play Store. Free Android app — no accounts, no ads, no tracking.

What is Patrimonia 360?

An independent Android app to bring you closer to the heritage that surrounds you, wherever you go.

🏛️

Discover heritage nearby

Six categories: churches, palaces, gardens, museums, monuments and viewpoints. With real photos, ratings and reviews.

🌍

UNESCO World Heritage map

Browse the entire UNESCO World Heritage list on an interactive world map. Works offline thanks to local caching.

🎧

Automatic audio guide

The app reads the Wikipedia article aloud while you walk to the place, so you arrive already informed. No subscription, no downloads.

🔒

Privacy first

No accounts, no advertising, no tracking, no analytics. Your location never leaves your device beyond the search itself.

Your privacy, our priority

Patrimonia 360 does not collect, store or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no advertising tracking, and no hidden analytics. Read our full Privacy Policy for the details.
